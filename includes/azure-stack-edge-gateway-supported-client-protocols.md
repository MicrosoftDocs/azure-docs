|**Protocol** |**Versions**   |**Notes**  |
|---------|---------|---------|
|SMB    | 2.X, 3.X      | SMB 1 isn't supported.|
|NFS |3.0, 4.1       |Mac OS is not supported with NFS v4.1.  |
