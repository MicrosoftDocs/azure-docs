> [!IMPORTANT]
> As of **August 31, 2024**, Azure classic administrator roles (along with Azure classic resources and Azure Service Manager) are retired and no longer supported. Starting in **December 2025**, Azure automatically assigned the Owner role at subscription scope to users in the public cloud who were still assigned the Co-Administrator or Service Administrator role. As of **May 2026**, classic administrator roles are fully retired and you must assign roles in [Azure role-based access control (RBAC)](/azure/role-based-access-control/built-in-roles) to manage access.
>
> For more information, see [Azure classic subscription administrators](/azure/role-based-access-control/classic-administrators).
