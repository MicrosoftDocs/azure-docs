Get started with proper architecture, and then learn how to optimize resources for your application. The [Service Fabric Patterns and Practices](https://mva.microsoft.com/en-US/training-courses/service-fabric-patterns-and-practices-16925?l=mudwqISGD_6005167344) course answers the questions most often asked by real-world customers about Service Fabric scenarios and application areas.
