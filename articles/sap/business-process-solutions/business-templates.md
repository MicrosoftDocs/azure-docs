# Templates for Business Process Solutions

This article introduces the suite of prebuilt Power BI reports and Copilot Studio agents that are available in Business Process Solutions. Key features and capabilities are highlighted. The templates support common business processes and offer immediate access to essential metrics and trends such as financial performance, sales effectiveness, and supplier evaluation. Organizations can quickly use these resources to gain valuable insights, with the flexibility to refine and customize them to suit specific business requirements.

## Finance Insights

:::image type="content" source="./media/business-templates/finance-insights-report.png" alt-text="Diagram that shows the Trial Balance report." lightbox="./media/business-templates/finance-insights-report.png":::

Finance Insights provide a comprehensive view of an organization's financial health and performance. It consolidates key financial data into a single reporting layer so that stakeholders can monitor, analyze, and interpret financial outcomes effectively.

Key capabilities supported by the Power BI report:

- Trial balance
- Year-to-date (YTD) trial balance
- YTD financial statement
- Profitability analysis

> [!NOTE]
> To use all the features on the Finance Insights, you need to replicate financial statement hierarchies into reporting core data services views. Check available SAP documentation and the Business Process Solutions troubleshooting guide.

## Sales and Billing Insights

:::image type="content" source="./media/business-templates/sales-billing-insights-dashboard.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows the Sales & Performance Report." lightbox="./media/business-templates/sales-billing-insights-dashboard.png":::

Sales and Billing Insights integrate sales order and billing data into a single reporting layer. Organizations can use Sales and Billing Insights to explore sales trends, monitor revenue, and assess performance across products, customers, and organizational structures.

Key capabilities supported by the Power BI report:

- Tracking of sales order volumes, values, and fulfillment status.
- Analysis of billing and revenue trends over time.
- Profit center-based reporting by using hierarchies.
- Support for core data translations to improve usability across languages and business units.

## Spend Insights

:::image type="content" source="./media/business-templates/spends-insights-report.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows Tail Spend Analysis." lightbox="./media/business-templates/spends-insights-report.png":::

Spend Insights provide a consolidated view of organizational expenditure across multiple dimensions. Spend Insights enable monitoring of spending behavior, identification of cost-saving opportunities, and assurance of compliance with procurement policies within a single reporting layer. Spend Insights incorporate currency exchange rates for accurate currency conversion and support analysis across categories, suppliers, and regions.

Key capabilities supported by the Power BI report:

- Overview of spend segmented by product category, supplier, and region.
- Spending patterns across multiple years with compliance checks against procurement standards.
- Comprehensive breakdown of spend across categories and suppliers for detailed cost analysis.
- Opportunities to consolidate suppliers and improve spending efficiency by addressing low-value, high-volume transactions.

## Accounts Payable Insights

:::image type="content" source="./media/business-templates/account-payables-report.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows Supplier Payments." lightbox="./media/business-templates/account-payables-report.png":::

Accounts Payable Insights give finance teams a clear view of outstanding obligations to suppliers and how these obligations affect outgoing cash flows. An aging view highlights overdue payables. Analysis by payment terms shows whether commitments are being settled according to agreed schedules. Accounts Payable Insights also reveal payment blocks that slow down processing and provide trend views that illustrate how payment volumes evolve over time.

Opportunities for early payment discounts are highlighted alongside supplier-level insights, such as balances and rankings of the top vendors. Accounts Payable Insights combine these perspectives to support better control over cash outflows, strengthen supplier relationships, and help to identify ways to optimize working capital.

Key capabilities supported by the Power BI report:

- Aging report
- Payment trends
- Supplier balances

## Accounts Receivable Insights

:::image type="content" source="./media/business-templates/account-receivables-report.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows the Aging Report." lightbox="./media/business-templates/account-receivables-report.png":::

Accounts Receivable Insights help organizations monitor the status of customer payments and understand how receivables affect cash flow. An aging view highlights overdue amounts and makes it easier to identify accounts at risk. Payment patterns can be analyzed in the context of agreed terms, showing whether customers settle invoices on time or require closer follow-up. Regional breakdowns reveal where overdue payments are concentrated. Customer balance views provide detailed information at the account level. Together, these perspectives give finance teams the tools to manage credit exposure more effectively and improve the predictability of incoming cash flows.

Key capabilities supported by the Power BI report:

- Aging report
- Customer balances

## Opportunity Insights

:::image type="content" source="./media/business-templates/opportunity-insights-dashboard.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows the Executive Summary." lightbox="./media/business-templates/opportunity-insights-dashboard.png":::

Opportunity Insights provide a unified view of the sales pipeline and performance metrics. Opportunity Insights consolidate opportunity data into a single reporting layer, enabling monitoring of pipeline health, deal progression, and revenue potential across the organization.

Key capabilities supported by the Power BI report:

- A snapshot of sales performance for the fiscal year that highlights key metrics like closed-won opportunities, total open pipeline value, and overall pipeline health.
- A structured view of the sales pipeline that showcases the progression of opportunities, overall deal values, and key performance indicators for effective pipeline management.

## Copilot agent

With agents, users can use natural language to interact with business processes and data. They can ask operational and analytical questions, and the agent, based on context, uses the data agent within Microsoft Fabric or sends a request directly to the source system. Beyond data retrieval, agents can act as system users. They can interact with applications for the user to perform tasks and reveal insights. They can also proactively provide recommendations, highlight anomalies, and guide decision-making.

:::image type="content" source="./media/business-templates/copilot-agent.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows the Copilot agent capabilities." lightbox="./media/business-templates/copilot-agent.png":::

More templates and connectors empower businesses to extend predelivered scenarios and design their own automations. Researcher and other deep reasoning agents can explore complex business questions by applying advanced reasoning and pattern recognition. Customers can use these agents to take a broad business perspective across their processes and act intelligently on new data.

For example, Researcher can analyze patterns across finance, sales, and procurement to uncover root causes of performance issues, identify dependencies between processes, or highlight opportunities for optimization. Agents combine this deeper analysis with real-time operational signals, which enables organizations to move from reactive reporting to proactive, insight-driven action.

:::image type="content" source="./media/business-templates/copilot-agent-tools.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows the Copilot agent tools." lightbox="./media/business-templates/copilot-agent-tools.png":::

## Summary

Business Process Solutions business templates offer a collection of prebuilt Power BI reports and Copilot Studio agents that help organizations turn data models into practical insights. The templates cover areas such as finance, sales, procurement, accounts payable and receivable, and sales opportunities. They provide immediate access to key metrics and trends. Users can customize and extend these resources to fit their business needs for more effective analysis and decision-making.
